Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 9, 2025

MusicApps4All respects your privacy. Our website is free to use and does not require sign-up, login, or downloads. We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

Information We May Collect

Like most websites, our hosting provider automatically logs basic technical details such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

This information is used only to keep the site secure and to improve performance. It is not used to personally identify you.

Cookies

MusicApps4All does not use cookies for tracking or advertising.

Some pages on our site include embedded YouTube videos. When you watch one of these videos, YouTube (a service provided by Google) may place cookies on your device. These cookies are used by YouTube to remember your preferences, measure viewing statistics, and support their own advertising system.

These cookies are controlled by Google, not by MusicApps4All. For more information, see Google’s Privacy Policy and Google’s Cookie Policy.

Children’s Privacy

MusicApps4All is safe for children and does not knowingly collect any personal information from users under 13.
